About ECOBAG Ventures

We're revolutionizing the packaging industry with sustainable solutions made from banana plant waste

Turning Waste into Eco-Friendly Packaging

At ECOBAG Ventures, we've developed innovative technology to transform banana plant stems - typically discarded as agricultural waste - into high-quality, biodegradable packaging materials.

Our mission is to reduce plastic pollution and provide businesses with sustainable packaging alternatives that don't compromise on quality or functionality.

With over 115 million tons of bananas produced annually, there's an abundance of raw material that can be repurposed into eco-friendly packaging solutions.

About Us

Our Story

ECOBAG Ventures was founded in 2019 by Chemical and Material engineers who witnessed firsthand the massive amounts of banana plant waste generated during harvest seasons. We saw an opportunity to transform this agricultural byproduct into something valuable.

What started as a university research project has grown into a mission-driven company dedicated to reducing plastic pollution and creating sustainable packaging alternatives.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the packaging industry by providing businesses with high-quality, biodegradable alternatives that reduce environmental impact without compromising functionality.

Our Vision

A world where packaging nourishes rather than pollutes the environment, creating a circular economy that benefits both businesses and the planet.

Our Technology

How we transform banana waste into sustainable packaging

From Banana Stem to Biodegradable Packaging

Our proprietary process transforms banana stems - typically discarded after harvest - into high-quality packaging materials that are both functional and environmentally responsible.

We work directly with banana farmers to collect the stems, which are then transported to our processing facilities. This creates an additional revenue stream for farmers while reducing agricultural waste.

The banana stems undergo a multi-step process to extract the valuable fibers and transform them into versatile packaging materials that meet industry standards for strength, durability, and functionality.
